Welcome to var'aq, the first language designed for the Klingon programmer!

var'aq is the first-ever attempt to get an idea of the technical culture and tools of Star Trek's Klingon Empire. It was motivated by a love of created languages and cultures, as well as a desire to see how far human programming paradigms can be stretched (and believe me, it can go a lot further...)

This page is a quick guide to the contents of the var'aq distribution as well as a link to the website.
